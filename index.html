Researcher. Observatorio San Calixto

I’m a multi-disciplined, product-oriented research geophysicist.

I specialise in instrumentation, wave propagation and imaging R&D industries, I was research-trained at France’s top schools, École Normale Supérieure and ESPCI at PSL University.

I'm coordinating seismology and infrasound programs at CEA for the CTBTO in Bolivia. With expertise spanning geophysics and medical imaging, I’ve tackled challenges like seismic data inversion at Sercel (publication in EAGE Near Surface Geophysics), distributed acoustic sensing microseismic analysis at Géoazur CNRS, and ultrasound device validation at Iconeus.

I have a proven track record for solving complex problems, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to advancing natural hazard monitoring, environmental understanding, and innovative technologies.

Current Research Areas

  • Machine learning automated workflow in temporary seismic networks.
  • Optimization of earthquake cluster relocation in seismotectonics.
  • Passive seismic tomography with Distributed Acoustic Sensing.

Recognition

My group's work has been recognized with an NSF CAREER Award and a Sloan Fellowship. We have won paper awards at conferences for NLP, Hardware, and Visualization, as well as awards for best demonstrations for open-source software.
