Geophysical and Instrumentation Network Engineer

I’m a multi-disciplined, product-oriented research geophysicist.

I specialise in instrumentation, wave propagation and imaging R&D industries, I was research-trained at France’s top schools, École Normale Supérieure and ESPCI at PSL University.

I'm supporting seismology and infrasound cooperation programs at CEA in Bolivia. With expertise spanning geophysics and medical imaging, I’ve tackled challenges like seismic data inversion at Sercel, distributed acoustic sensing microseismic analysis at Géoazur CNRS, and ultrasound device validation at Iconeus.

I have a proven track record for solving complex problems, I bring a multidisciplinary approach to advancing natural hazard monitoring, environmental understanding, and innovative technologies.

Current Research Areas

  • Machine learning detection in temporary seismic networks.
  • Earthquake clustering and relocation
  • Passive seismic tomography and beamforming with Distributed Acoustic Sensing.
